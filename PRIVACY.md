# Privacy Policy — Cashflow Monitor

**Last updated: 2026-07-06**

Cashflow Monitor is a local-only Chrome extension for personal cashflow, bill, and account
balance tracking. This policy explains what data the extension handles and how.

## Data collection

Cashflow Monitor does **not** collect, transmit, sell, or share any user data with the
developer or any third party. The extension has no backend server, makes no network
requests, and includes no analytics, advertising, or tracking code of any kind.

## Data storage

All data you enter — accounts, income sources, bills, transfers, and settings — is stored
**locally on your device only**, using the browser's built-in `chrome.storage.local` API.
This data:

- Never leaves your device.
- Is not accessible to the developer or anyone else.
- Persists only in your local Chrome profile, and is removed if you uninstall the
  extension or clear its storage.

## Permissions

| Permission | Why it's needed |
|---|---|
| `storage` | Persist the accounts, income, bills, transfers, and settings you enter, locally on your device. |
| `sidePanel` | Let the same interface be opened in Chrome's side panel, in addition to a full browser tab. |

Neither permission is used to access, read, or transmit any data beyond what the extension
itself stores for your own use.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised accordingly.

## Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the developer via the extension's
Chrome Web Store listing page.
